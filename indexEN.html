About the Company

The company was founded in May 6th, 2020 by Cleber Rodrigues de Souza, aiming to supply the need of companies and professionals in technology area. Since his childhood, Cleber shown an interest in technology and saw his future in it. As he got older, people began to show interest in the same idea were gathered.

Thereby, Alessandra, Cleber, Eduardo, Leonardo, Railson and Ruthe began the corporation’s development, creating websites, systems and databases. Since that day, we have worked to contribute more and more to the technology market.


Mission and Values

Our greatest aspiration as professionals, and especially as people, is to reach all kinds of audiences as much as possible, so that they can be aware of the capability that technology together with our company can achieve. Thus, seeking to grow in the market, as well as in the contact with the various types of audiences.

Our priorities are to attend our entire public with a series of values aim the commitment to the client, always solving all their needs:

  • - Trust and respect for everyone, regardless of position or relationship;
  • - Love what we do;
  • - Offer a good working environment;
  • - Prioritize customer satisfaction and the quality of our services;
  • - Always keep the ethics;
  • - Professional improvement.
